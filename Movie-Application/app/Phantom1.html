Description:

The movie is an action-packed thriller based on a series of attacks that terrorized Mumbai and the whole nation. In his quest to seek justice, Daniyal, the male protagonist travels across the world, from India to Europe, America and the Middle East. As Daniyal sets out on his journey, he learns the hard way that life is not always fair and everybody has to pay a price for accomplishment of certain missions and he is no exception. The movie is high on action and traces the tricky journey of Daniyal as he is determined to fulfill his mission.
