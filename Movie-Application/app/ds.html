Description:

Highlighting a family-struggle, Double Seat is about a recently married couple, Amit, played by Chaudhari, and Manjiri, played by Barve wish to move out of the family home and buy their own house. While Amit was born and brought up in metropolitan Mumbai, Manjiri is from the village of Roha and has difficulty coping with city life. Amit works in the packing business and is chained to this reality by his father, who trains horses. Manjiri, on the other hand has a zest for life, and is optimistic while working as a LIC agent
